Our Leadership

Dr. P Murthuza Khan

MBBS, MD, ABIM MRCP(Endocrinology and Diabetes)

Dr. Pathan Murthuza Khan, a distinguished medical professional with an illustrious 45 years of experience in the healthcare sector. Dr. Khan is a seasoned consultant internist and endocrinologist, specializing in the management of all types of medical and endocrine emergencies, as well as elective cases.
Educational Background:
Dr. Khan holds an impressive educational background, having earned his MBBS and MD degrees from Kurnool Medical College. He is also certified by the American Board of Internal Medicine (ABIM) and holds memberships with the Royal College of Physicians (MRCP) in both Endocrinology and Diabetes in the United Kingdom.
Teaching Excellence:
Beyond his clinical practice, Dr. Khan is a dedicated educator, contributing significantly to the field of internal medicine, endocrinology, and diabetes. He has actively participated in the planning and execution of residency and fellowship programs, sharing his extensive knowledge and fostering the growth of future medical professionals.
Research Prowess:
Dr. Khan's commitment to advancing medical knowledge is evident through his prolific research endeavors. He has authored approximately 40 research articles in peer-reviewed journals, contributing valuable insights to the medical and endocrine communities.
Administrative Acumen:
Known for his exceptional administrative, organizational, and quality control skills, Dr. Khan brings a strategic approach to healthcare management. His proficiency in overseeing operations ensures efficiency and high standards in medical practice.
