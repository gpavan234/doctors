Who We Are?

Welcome to Dr. Khan's Diabetes Care, Endocrinology, and Internal Medicine Center—your hub for personalized healthcare both offline and online. Our dedicated team, led by Dr. Khan's, specializes in managing diabetes, endocrine disorders, and internal medical conditions.

We not only consult patients online but also have the capability to access continuous glucose monitoring (CGM) data, allowing us to suggest personalized corrective plans for optimal health.

Experience compassionate expertise and comprehensive care from anywhere with Dr. Khan's Center.

Navigating Diabetes: What You Need to Know

Welcome to Dr. Khan's Diabetes Care Center, where our dedicated team of experts focuses on personalized guidance for effective diabetes management. From customized nutrition plans to lifestyle recommendations, we are committed to empowering you on your journey to optimal health.

Diet Support

Collaborating with our in-house nutritionists and dietitians ensures a tailored approach to weight management and healthy eating habits.

Fitness

We offer personalized fitness training and counseling to aid in insulin management and stress reduction for comprehensive health support.

Preventive Care

Through routine check-ups and timely interventions, we proactively work to prevent the onset of diabetes and mitigate potential complications.

Precision Diabetes

Our approach personalizes healthcare, adapting testing, decisions, and treatments to each individual's unique needs.

Stress Management

We conduct psychological assessments and offer patient education, skills development, and motivation through counseling.

Home Care

Placing our patients' well-being at the forefront of our services, receive top-notch treatment and care in the comfort of your own home.

Our Process

Step 1.
Comprehensive Consultation

Begin with a detailed consultation where our experienced team, led by Dr. Khan's, gathers information about your medical history, lifestyle, and specific health concerns.

Step 2.
Diagnostic Evaluation

Utilize state-of-the-art diagnostics, including advanced blood tests and imaging, to obtain a thorough understanding of your condition and its underlying factors.

Step 3.
Personalized Treatment Plan

Tailor a comprehensive treatment plan that addresses not only the symptoms but also the root causes, incorporating the latest advancements in diabetes management, endocrinology, internal medicine, and dietary guidance.

Step 4.
Dietary Assessment

Work closely with our nutritionists to assess your dietary habits, ensuring that your nutritional intake aligns with your health goals and complements your overall treatment plan.

Step 5.
Online and Offline Consultations

Offer the flexibility of both offline and online consultations, providing seamless access to our expertise from the comfort of your home or in-person at our center.

Step 6.
Continuous Glucose Monitoring (CGM)

Employ cutting-edge technology to access continuous glucose monitoring (CGM) data, allowing for real-time insights into your glucose levels and facilitating precise adjustments to your treatment and dietary plan.

Step 7.
Patient Educational Support

Empower patients with knowledge through educational programs, ensuring a deep understanding of their condition, dietary needs, and equipping them with the tools for active participation in their health journey.

Step 8.
Regular Follow-ups

Implement a structured follow-up system to monitor progress, make necessary adjustments to the treatment and dietary plans, and address any emerging concerns, ensuring ongoing support and care.
