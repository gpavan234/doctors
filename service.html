Our Services

Consultation

Our expert team provides personalized consultations to assess your diabetic condition, address concerns, and devise a comprehensive management plan tailored to your needs.

Diabetic Educator Support

Receive guidance and support from our diabetic educators who offer valuable insights, practical tips, and ongoing education to help you understand and manage your diabetes better.

Continuous Glucose Monitoring (CGM) Application and Analysis

Utilize the latest technology in continuous glucose monitoring with our assistance in applying CGM devices and analyzing the data to gain valuable insights into your blood sugar trends and patterns.

Diet Planning

Our certified dietitians specialize in creating customized meal plans that align with your dietary preferences and health goals, empowering you to manage your diabetes effectively through nutrition.

Support Groups

Join our supportive community of individuals living with diabetes to share experiences, gain encouragement, and access valuable resources for navigating the challenges of diabetes management together.

Retina Checkup

Protect your vision with regular retina checkups conducted by our specialized eye care professionals, aimed at detecting and managing diabetic retinopathy, a common complication of diabetes.

Erectile Dysfunction Test

Address the sensitive issue of erectile dysfunction by undergoing comprehensive testing and receiving personalized treatment recommendations from our empathetic healthcare providers.

Cardiovascular Services

Safeguard your heart health with our range of cardiovascular services, including risk assessments, diagnostic tests, and lifestyle interventions tailored to reduce your risk of heart disease associated with diabetes.

Foot Care Management

Prevent complications such as diabetic foot ulcers and neuropathy by availing of our specialized foot care services, including regular assessments, education on proper foot care, and timely interventions.

Medication Management

Receive expert guidance on medication options, dosage adjustments, and adherence strategies to effectively manage your diabetes and prevent complications while optimizing your overall well-being.
